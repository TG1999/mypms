ABOUT US

This website facilitates finding the best locality for an industry setup depending on the ir quality wholly. It comprises of the database of all the industries in particular locality and parts per million concentration(ppm) of toxic gases being emitted from it, if any. It is then evaluated and updated according to the threshold standard set by the Ministry of Environment. If industrial pollution lies within this threshold level, industrialist get the land availabilities in the region or other best places are suggested accordingly, keeping factors and government policies in consideration.
